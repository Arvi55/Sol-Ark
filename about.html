About Mission Solark

AI-driven space weather intelligence.

SOL-ARK is a machine learning-powered platform that analyzes solar and geomagnetic data to forecast space weather events and assess their potential impact on satellites and communication systems. Our mission is to provide early warnings and meaningful insights to help mitigate the risks posed by solar storms.
